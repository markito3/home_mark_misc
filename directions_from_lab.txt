From the Lab:

1. South on Jefferson
2. Right on to J. Clyde Morris Blvd.
3. Left on to Warwick Blvd. (that's south on Warwick)
4. Go past the 7-11 on your right (corner of Warwick and Cedar Ave.)
5. Turn right at the next street, Elm Ave. There is a Texaco gas station on
   the near right corner.
6. Go past Boutetourt
7. Go past Pin Oak (4-way stop)
8. Turn left on to Holly Drive

From the interstate:

1. Take the J. Clyde Morris exit going west.
2. Pick up the directions at (3) above.
